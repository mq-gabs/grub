Welcome to Grub – Your Digital Menu Solution

MyMenu is an innovative online menu service that helps restaurants, cafés, and food trucks create and share their menus digitally. Whether you're looking to go paperless or just want to make menu updates easier and faster, we’ve got you covered.

With Grub, you can easily:

  • Create and update your menu items anytime
  • Organize items into categories like appetizers, main courses, desserts, and drinks
  • Display your menu beautifully on any device
  • Share your menu via QR codes or direct links
